Motivation

The main motivation for wanting to increase my personal and professional productivity stems from the fact that time is valuable and I should be looking to maximise what I can achieve with mine. I also believe that the people who build the quickest and learn the fastest will ultimately "win" in the business sense, with that in mind I aspire to go faster.

Like me you have probably read dozens of blog posts about a wide variety of ways to increase your productivity from business processes to meditation. Here I will attempt, where possible, to experiment with the latest productivity research and report tangible results i.e. hours saved or additional lines of code shipped.

Some of the lifestyle and work areas I plan to experiment with:

  • Alcohol consumption
  • Hydration
  • Diet
  • Sleep pattern
  • Exercise
  • Stretching
  • Meditation
  • Reading techniques
  • Interruptions/Notifications
  • Work decisions - platforms and reusable code
  • Delegation and recruitment
  • Nurturing beneficial habits
